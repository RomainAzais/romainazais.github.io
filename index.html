Presentation

I am a research scientist in applied mathematics at Inria Lyon, France.

Main research interests

  • Inference and simulation of stochastic processes
  • Learning on combinatorial structures
  • Applications in geography and social sciences

Short bio

  • Since April 2025, I am an Associate Editor for the three series of Communications in Statistics: Theory and Methods; Simulation and Computation; Case Studies, Data Analysis and Applications.
  • Since 2022, I am an elected member of the mathematical statistics group of the French Statistical Society. I am its president since Summer 2024.
  • I defended my habilitation thesis at ENS de Lyon in December 2022.
  • I defended my PhD thesis at Université Bordeaux 1 in July 2013.
